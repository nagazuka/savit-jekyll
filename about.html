Our philosophy

Savit Consultancy aims to provide high-end enterprise integration consultancy services for clients in any sector. We have experience in challenging projects for large-scale and complex integration platforms. We want to use this experience to help our clients achieve their enterprise integration goals.

Our place

Savit Consultancy is located in Richmond, part of Greater London. From this location, we can serve clients in the entire London area. For short-term projects, it is possible to contract Savit Consultancy for international projects.
